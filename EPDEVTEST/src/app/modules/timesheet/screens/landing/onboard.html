What’s New?

  • Timesheet approval is now two-level. Approval is done by your Delivery Manager & Administration Manager
  • A new feature, “Same as previous week/ month” for timesheets is added
  • New options, “Leave & Holiday” and “Non-Billable” are added to the dropdown under projects & SOW
  • Attachments can view & Delete easily
  • Total submitted hours and approved hours display in the respective project
  • Default hours are self-loaded if you are assigned with a single SOW/Project. Just verify before you apply.
  • Managers can download “Timesheet Report”
  • Manager can switch to previous and next week/month timesheets of associates
  • Manager can view individual associate details
  • Enhanced user interface and improved performance
